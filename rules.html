📖 How to Play

1
The Deck is Dealt
All 146 IPL 2021 player cards are shuffled and split evenly between you and your opponent. You can only see your own top card.
2
Choose Your Stat
Click a stat on your card to challenge your opponent. You have 20 seconds — the timer auto-picks your best stat if it expires.
3
Comparison Rules
Higher wins: Matches, Runs, Highest Score, Average, Strike Rate, 100s, 50s, 4s, 6s, Wickets.

Lower wins (▼): Economy Rate & Bowling Average — fewer runs given = better.

If a bowler stat shows N/A it means that player doesn't bowl — you can't select it.
4
Card Transfer
The winner takes both cards. On a draw, both go to a communal pot won by the next round's victor. The winner of each round chooses the next stat.
5
Win the Match
First player to hold all cards wins. Results are saved to the Leaderboard. In multiplayer, both players see results live via Firebase.
